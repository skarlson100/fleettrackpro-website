Features

Everything your fleet needs

Purpose-built tools for Australian fleet operators. No fluff — just what matters on the job.

AI Receipt Scanning

Photograph receipts and our AI extracts vendor, amount and date automatically. Zero manual data entry — ever.

GPS Trip Tracking

Every journey logged automatically. Tag trips to jobs and vehicles with a single tap. Full route history, always available.

Compliance Management

Registration, licence and inspection deadlines tracked in one place. Automated reminders keep your fleet road-legal, always.

PDF Reports

Generate professional maintenance and expense reports in seconds. Ready for your accountant, fleet manager or insurance claim.

Multi-User Roles

Drivers see only their vehicles. Mechanics manage service records. Fleet managers see everything. Granular permissions, zero complexity.

Service Interval Alerts

Distance and time-based alerts ensure scheduled maintenance never slips. Your vehicles stay in peak condition, your liability stays low.
