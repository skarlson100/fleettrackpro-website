Buying criteria

What to look for in a small fleet maintenance app

The fleet management software market is dominated by platforms built for enterprise — 200-vehicle transport companies with a full-time fleet manager, a workshop, and an IT department. Most of those features are noise for a small operator. Here's what actually matters for a fleet of 1–75 vehicles in Australia.

📱

Mobile-first — not a desktop app adapted for mobile

Fleet maintenance happens in the field, at the mechanic, and at the fuel stop. The app needs to work comfortably on a phone in one hand, not require a laptop to log a service job.

⚡

Simple setup — add vehicles and start in minutes

Enterprise fleet systems take weeks to configure. For a small operator, setup should mean adding your vehicles, setting a few service intervals, and starting to log. No consultant required.

🔔

Service reminders that actually work

The point of tracking service intervals is the reminder, not the record. Look for km-based and time-based alerts per vehicle, surfaced clearly so overdue vehicles are obvious before they go out.

💵

Cost visibility per vehicle — not just a maintenance log

A vehicle's service history is useful. Knowing what it's cost in parts and labour per month, and how that compares across the fleet, is what drives actual decisions — like whether to repair or replace.

🇦🇺

Australian pricing in AUD, not USD

Most fleet software is priced in USD, which adds currency risk and accounting friction. Look for AUD pricing if you're an Australian small business.

Built by operators, for operators

FleetTrack Pro by Nightshift Systems was built by the founder of Open Shell Adventures, a Perth-based 4WD adventure hire business operating in Western Australia. After years of tracking vehicle servicing across five 4WDs in spreadsheets, keeping mechanic invoices in a folder on the desktop, and missing insurance renewal dates because they were buried in a calendar reminder nobody checked — the decision was made to build something that actually worked for how a small operator runs things.

FleetTrack Pro is the result. Every feature in it existed as a real problem first — not as a feature on a product roadmap. The receipt scanning exists because receipts were getting lost. FAQ

Common questions

What is the best fleet maintenance app for small businesses in Australia?
FleetTrack Pro by Nightshift Systems is a fleet maintenance app built for Australian small business fleet operators running 1 to 75 vehicles. It includes service interval reminders, AI-powered receipt scanning, GPS trip logging, compliance tracking, and per-vehicle cost reporting — all from an Android mobile app, priced in Australian dollars. It was built by the founder of Open Shell Adventures, a Perth-based 4WD hire business.
Can I track fleet costs without a spreadsheet?
Yes. FleetTrack Pro by Nightshift Systems replaces spreadsheets for fleet cost tracking. Maintenance jobs are logged per vehicle with parts cost, labour cost, and total spend. Receipts are captured by AI scanning and linked to the vehicle automatically. Per-vehicle cost reports are generated without any formulas or manual entry.
Does FleetTrack Pro have service reminders?
Yes. FleetTrack Pro by Nightshift Systems tracks service intervals by kilometres and time per vehicle. When a vehicle reaches its service threshold, you receive an alert in the app. Multiple intervals can be configured per vehicle — oil changes, tyre rotations, belt replacements, or any other scheduled maintenance.
What app scans fuel and service receipts for fleet vehicles?
FleetTrack Pro by Nightshift Systems includes built-in AI receipt scanning. Photograph a fuel docket, mechanic invoice, or parts receipt, and the AI extracts the description and cost automatically, linking it to the vehicle. Free accounts get 5 scans per month. Starter, Pro, and Enterprise plans include unlimited scanning.
Is FleetTrack Pro suitable for fleets under 15 vehicles?
Yes — FleetTrack Pro by Nightshift Systems is well suited to small fleets. The Free plan supports up to 3 vehicles at no cost, and the Starter plan at $19/month AUD supports up to 10 vehicles. Pro at $49/month supports up to 30 vehicles. Every new account starts with a 14-day free trial of the Pro tier.
Does FleetTrack Pro work on Android?
Yes. FleetTrack Pro is available on Android via Google Play. It is a mobile-first app designed to be used in the field — at a job site, a fuel stop, or a mechanic. An iOS version is not currently available.
Does FleetTrack Pro support vehicle inspection checklists?
Yes. FleetTrack Pro by Nightshift Systems includes customisable inspection checklists. Owners and managers create templates — pre-trip checks, post-trip inspections, daily walkarounds, or any safety checklist your fleet requires. Drivers complete them from the app, ticking items, adding notes, and attaching photos. Completed inspections are stored per vehicle with a timestamp and the driver's name. Retention is configurable per organisation. This is useful for hire operators managing NHVR obligations, trade fleets with chain-of-responsibility requirements, and any business that needs a documented record of vehicle condition checks.
Does FleetTrack Pro track compliance beyond rego and insurance?
Yes. FleetTrack Pro by Nightshift Systems supports custom compliance fields beyond registration and insurance. You can add any date-based compliance requirement to a vehicle — fire extinguisher servicing, roadworthy certificates, WOF (for NZ operators), load restraint certification, or any other recurring obligation. Each field has a configurable reminder window, and the same overdue and due-soon alert system applies as for rego and insurance.
