Who we are

FleetTrack Pro is built by Steven Karlson, founder of Open Shell Adventures, a Perth-based 4WD adventure hire business. The app started as an internal tool to manage a small fleet of Hilux and Ranger 4WDs — and grew into something we thought other operators could use too.

Nightshift Systems is the company behind FleetTrack Pro. We're a small team based in Perth, Western Australia.
